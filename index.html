Argument

Why Food Suppliers Outside Are Not Important

Fristly

Supports Student Traders Families

By allowing students to manage the canteen, it creates an opportunity for students who come from merchant families to practice entrepreneurship. This system can directly benefit students by giving them a space to contribute to their family's business while learning essential business skills.

Secondly

Better Hygiene and Health Standards

When students run the canteen, the school can closely monitor the hygiene and quality of the food. Relying on external suppliers can introduce risks, as the school has less control over food safety, which could negatively affect students' health.

Finally

Platform for Business Learning

The school canteen serves as an ideal place for students to apply the entrepreneurship lessons from their PKK classes. Managing the canteen independently allows them to practice product development, marketing, and financial management in a real-world setting.

Reiteration

In summary, student management of SMKN 11 Malang's canteen offers more than just food service. It creates a practical entrepreneurship training ground, supporting students from trading families and ensuring better food standards.

Importantly, it transforms the canteen into a live classroom for applying business theory. This approach equips students with valuable skills for future business ventures, making the canteen an integral part of their educational journey.
